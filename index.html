The Problem

AI can process transactions, reconcile accounts, and summarize results. But it cannot design a balanced financial future unless accounting structure and forecasting logic are unified.

Most systems separate the General Ledger (history) from FP&A models (future). That structural gap forces reconciliation, limits forecasting accuracy, and prevents AI from reasoning intelligently.

The Solution

bot-Planner creates a continuous financial model across time — linking historical accounting and forward forecasting inside one standardized framework.

One financial structure. One evolving set of accounts. Forecasts remain balanced. AI becomes more powerful. Finance professionals move from calculation to imagination.
